## Failure Modes

The most common way small talent collectives die is **supply without paid demand**. Operators assemble an impressive room of engineers, but buyers are not pre-sold, roles are vague, and the founder becomes an unpaid career coach and connector. The second failure mode is the **founder bottleneck**. The third failure mode is **trust erosion**. A curated technical room becomes worthless when low-quality candidates are pushed, sponsors are irrelevant, or recruiter behavior gets spammy.
