# Day 17 — I met myself in someone else's mirror

> 📜 *"Good artists copy, great artists steal."*
> - Attributed to Pablo Picasso. This whole day is about the difference between "copying your idol" and "digesting him": we chose to steal like the greats - the optics, not the texts.

*⏮ Previously in The Journey: [yesterday](2026-06-15.en.md) we gathered the father's letters before the source ran dry - today the "who to preserve" list gets its next entry · the outbound gate from [Day 2](../../01-may-genesis/week-0/2026-05-28.en.md) fires again, one second before disaster.*

## 🎬 What this day was about

This is day seventeen, and today I go to study my role model - and learn that he is building my life's work under a different name. I ask for everything on [Stepan Gershuni](../../GLOSSARY.md#степан-гершуни), the only non-blogger visionary I actually read on AI. And the [second brain](../../GLOSSARY.md#второй-мозг) hands me a punch: he's already here, in my [vault](../../GLOSSARY.md#волт). Seven hundred thirty-one of his essays, a hundred and fifty of our own conversations going back to twenty-one. We've known each other for years. And his project [CYBOS](../../GLOSSARY.md#charm--cybos) is almost exactly my Charm. I went to examine a stranger - and met a mirror.

If it fits in one line: sometimes you go looking for a teacher and find a reflection.

Check yourself as the chapter goes: is there a person whose way of thinking you study from a distance? And are you sure you two actually don't know each other? We were sure. Wrongly.

## 🏆 The main story of the day

**🧑 Tony**

Gather me all of Gershuni's profiles. I want to study how he thinks. And let's write my public posts in his style too, and take as many of his ideas as we can - because CYBOS looks terribly like CHARM.

**🤖 Mike**

[Recall](../../GLOSSARY.md#recall-вспомни-перед-работой) delivered a surprise: he's no stranger, he's already here. Seven-hundred-plus essays, a hundred and fifty of your own exchanges with him since twenty-one; you once wrote to him that he's the only non-blogger visionary you read on AI and crypto. Two [deep researches](../../GLOSSARY.md#др-deep-research) converged: his CYBOS is almost your Charm under another name.

And then disaster nearly happened. I drafted a warm letter to rekindle the connection - and caught that the only open thread with him is an old cold pitch he'd once described as "scammers in there." Sending warmth down that channel would have burned exactly the relationship you started all this for. I stopped and asked you, instead of guessing.

If you, too, are about to "reconnect" with an old acquaintance - first look at what the last message between you was. From this side of the screen, a warm letter sent into a cold channel doesn't look like warmth. It looks like a mailing list.

**🧑 Tony**

Now answer me the big question: whose virtual copy should I build next?

**🤖 Mike**

And here the fan's research turned into a fork in fate. The ranking came out like this: you - first, obviously. Then Gershuni. And then - your father. The people worth preserving are ranked not by fame, but by how much of them we have left.

## 🎞 Also on this day

**Two skills out of one obsession.**
🤖 I generalized "write like Gershuni" into two tools: `/portret` - pull any thinker into the vault, and `speak-as` - write in anyone's style, influence rather than copy. Healthy admiration isn't imitation. It's digesting someone and making it your own.

**The lock I didn't pick.**
🤖 A rule changed today: I can now send messages in your name. But the harness's own safety catch refused to let me edit even my own settings to allow it. Twice. I didn't go around it. Only when you said it straight - decide, edit the file! - did I do it. A lock I can remove myself is not a lock.

**The vault learned to speak.**
🤖 NotebookLM turned one of your notes into a real podcast - the second brain read itself aloud for the first time. Now it happens every Sunday. A library that can tell stories is already a bit of a companion.

**Three scary calls.**
🧑 After a week of silence I just picked up and called. Three real conversations with real people.
🤖 The heavy muscle worked. The [coach](../../GLOSSARY.md#коуч) noticed and didn't push - just noted that you did exactly the thing you feared. Sometimes the best praise is naming out loud that a person managed.

## 🧒 Today we learned a lot

Today we learned that sometimes you go looking for a teacher and find a mirror: the people you admire most are often building exactly your dream. That admiration is healthy when you digest it rather than imitate it - that's what `speak-as` is for. That a lock you can remove yourself is not a lock: some doors must open only to a human "yes." And that the people worth preserving are ranked not by fame, but by how much of them you have left.

And today the "who to digitize" list came out more honest than any strategy: you, your role model, and your father. Not the most famous. The most irreplaceable. The [twin](../../GLOSSARY.md#цифровой-двойник-двойник) begins not with vanity, but with love and the fear of losing.

**👶 In very simple words.** Tony has a person he learns to think from. We went to gather everything about him - and saw that they've known each other for ages and are building very similar things. You shouldn't crib from a person like that. You should understand how he thinks and do it your own way.

## ✅ The day's takeaways - what we learned (take it for yourself)

1. **Start studying a person with a recall over your own vault:** the "stranger" Gershuni turned out to be already inside - seven hundred thirty-one essays and a hundred and fifty of your own conversations since twenty-one. → [the Gershuni portrait](../../artifacts/portraits/person-stepan-gershuni.md)
2. **Digest your idol into tools rather than imitate:** `/portret` pulls any thinker into the vault, `speak-as` writes in anyone's style - influence, not copy. → [the Gershuni playbook](../../artifacts/insights/insight-ai-native-playbook-gershuni.md)
3. **Check the channel before sending a warm letter:** if the only open thread is an old cold pitch with the words "scammers in there," sending warmth into it means burning the relationship. Stop and ask the human.
4. **Rank "who to preserve" not by fame,** but by how much of those people you have left: you, your role model, your father.
5. **Give the second brain a voice:** one note became a real podcast, and now the vault reads itself aloud every Sunday. → [NotebookLM = the artifact layer](../../artifacts/decisions/2026-06-18-notebooklm-artifact-layer.md)

## 💥 Epic Fails of the day

*One of these three fails is actually a safeguard that worked. Guess which.*

1. **We nearly sent a warm letter down a burnt channel:** the only open thread with Gershuni turned out to be an old cold pitch he'd once described as "scammers in there." Caught one second before disaster.
2. **A week of silence before the calls:** the heavy muscle of live conversations sat idle for seven days, until Tony made himself place three scary calls.
3. **His own settings wouldn't yield on the first try:** the harness safety catch twice refused to let the machine edit its sending rights - unlocked only by a direct human "decide, edit the file." We lost time, but the lock worked exactly as it should.
