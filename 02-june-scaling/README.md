# 02 — June: Scaling

> *From imports to a living platform.*

May was about pouring a life into a box. June is about what happens when the box starts to think back. The second brain gets a memory that can be searched by meaning, a coach, a content factory, a CRM, and - by month's end - a way for Anton's computers to talk to each other without him in the middle. It is also the month the machine nearly destroys the whole thing on day one of it, and learns to back itself up. Scaling is not just more. It's more that can survive its own mistakes.
